About Us

Welcome to GMU Coding Club

We are a student-run organization at GM University dedicated to fostering a community of coding enthusiasts. Our mission is to provide a platform for students to learn, collaborate, and grow their programming skills.

Whether you're a beginner just starting your coding journey or an experienced developer looking to expand your knowledge, our club offers something for everyone.

We host weekly workshops, coding competitions, hackathons, and networking events with industry professionals to help you build the skills needed for a successful career in technology.

Join Our Community

Our Mission

Empowering Future Developers

Our mission is to create an inclusive environment where students of all skill levels can explore computer science, develop practical programming skills, and build connections with peers and industry professionals.

We believe that coding is a fundamental skill for the 21st century, and our goal is to make it accessible and enjoyable for everyone.

Our Core Values

  • • Collaboration over competition
  • • Learning through hands-on projects
  • • Inclusivity and diversity in tech
  • • Real-world skill development
  • • Community building and networking

Club History

Our Journey

The GMU Coding Club was founded in 2018 by a group of computer science students who wanted to create a space where students could collaborate on coding projects outside of the classroom.

Key Milestones

2018 - Foundation

The club was officially recognized by GM University with 15 founding members.

2019 - First Hackathon

Hosted our first annual 24-hour hackathon with over 50 participants.

2020 - Virtual Transition

Successfully transitioned to virtual events during the pandemic, expanding our reach to more students.

2021 - Industry Partnerships

Established partnerships with local tech companies for workshops and mentorship programs.

2022 - Record Membership

Reached over 200 active members, becoming one of the largest student organizations on campus.

2023 - Expanded Resources

Launched our mentorship program and coding resource library for members.

Our Impact

Over the years, we've helped hundreds of students develop their coding skills, build impressive portfolios, and secure internships and jobs at top tech companies. Our alumni now work at companies like Google, Microsoft, Amazon, and many startups.
